Empowering Deaf Individuals Through Education

Learn English and Computer Basics with Accessible Courses

Welcome to our project aimed at bridging the communication gap for deaf individuals. This system provides an accessible platform for learning English and computer basics in sign language, enhancing career prospects and personal growth. Our modules integrate HamNoSys (Hamburg Notation System) and SiGML (Signing Gesture Markup Language) avatars, delivering content in an interactive, visually engaging format to help users understand complex topics. Each lecture concludes with multiple-choice questions (MCQs) to assess comprehension and reinforce learning. Our goal is to empower deaf individuals with the skills they need to succeed in a digital world.
